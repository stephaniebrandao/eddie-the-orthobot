FAQ

Who is Eddie?
Eddie is an AI-powered assistant designed to help orthodontists simplify case management, generate reports, and provide smart suggestions for treatment protocols.

How can Eddie make my work easier?
Eddie automates repetitive tasks like drafting clinical reports, organizing patient data, and creating checklists, so you can spend more time focusing on your patients.

Do I need technical skills to use Eddie?
Preferably yes, but it's okay if you don't. Eddie is designed to be intuitive. You create an account, upload your case details, and Eddie will help you with the rest.

Can Eddie analyze patient images or X-rays?
Currently, Eddie focuses on text-based case support and report generation. Image analysis is planned as a future feature.

Is Eddie secure for patient information?
Yes. Eddie follows strict data protection standards to keep patient information safe and confidential.
