About Eddie

Eddie is an AI-powered assistant created to support orthodontic professionals in their daily work.

By automating repetitive tasks such as drafting clinical reports, organizing patient data, and creating checklists, Eddie helps you focus on what matters most — delivering excellent care to your patients.

Designed with simplicity and security in mind, Eddie combines intuitive technology with strict data protection standards to make your workflow smoother, smarter, and safer.


Fun Facts About Eddie

A Name with History

Eddie's name was inspired by Dr. Edward H. Angle, widely recognized as the “father of modern orthodontics.”

Bow Tie Personality

Eddie's little bow tie symbolizes professionalism with a friendly twist, reminding you that technology can be both smart and approachable.

Always Learning

While Eddie currently focuses on text-based case support, future updates will expand his skills, so he grows alongside the needs of orthodontic professionals.
